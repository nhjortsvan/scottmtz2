Nuevo Mazatlán- Pueblo Bonito
Tennis Camp
December 4-11, 2017

Scott Borowiak Tennis Tours is happy to announce a NEW venue for our tennis camp! Enjoy a week of high-level instruction and social play at the magnificent Pueblo Bonito Resort located on beautiful Emerald Bay in Nuevo Mazatlán. In addition to our resort, we will be taking advantage of other local tennis venues for our clinics and social play. Free shuttle service provided.
Now is the time to improve your game, make new friends and enjoy intimate on court lessons and social play! Plan for a dynamic week of play and instruction designed to maximize your tennis experience and address your personal goals.
     
 The $595 Tennis Option (per person) includes Daily morning and Afternoon Tennis .

• Daily Two- hour classes, six mornings at Pueblo Bonito or El Cid 
• Group size will vary. Our goal is 3 to 5 people per court.
• Afternoon Social Match play Round-Robin Sessions
• Learn strategy and technique for singles doubles.
• Daily “Pro of the Day” presentation/clinic for the entire group.

Players will be assigned to groups based on NTRP ratings.
